    Empowering Women Through Financial Freedom

    At Aakanksha Group Services, we are on a mission to uplift women by providing them with easy access to essential household products. Our unique financing model is designed exclusively for women, helping them take charge of their financial independence while ensuring a seamless and structured repayment process.

    How It Works?

    Step 1: Small Group Formation

    A minimum of five women come together to form a group.

    This group serves as a support system, ensuring timely repayments and financial discipline.

    Step 2: Avail Household Products

    Once the group is formed, members can choose household products they need.

    These products are financed exclusively in the name of women, even with Zero Downpayment, empowering them directly.

    Step 3: Weekly Installments & Collection

    Every 7 days, each group member pays a fixed installment amount on a certain day.

    A center leader (chosen from the group) is responsible for collecting payments from all members.

    The center leader then hands over the collected amount to the field officer, assigned to their region.

    Step 4: Dedicated Field Officers

    Our field officers play a key role in facilitating smooth transactions.

    They provide financial guidance, collect payments, and ensure that members have a hassle-free experience.

    Step 5: Rewards & Recognition

    We value the efforts of our center leaders and recognize their contribution.

    Every year, center leaders are rewarded with special gifts as a token of appreciation for their dedication and hard work.
