Our Journey of Growth

2020
The Humble Beginning
Aakanksha Group started operations with a single center and a small group of 6 courageous women in Birpur, Begusarai. In 2020, during a time of global uncertainty, we defied the lockdowns to prove that trust is stronger than fear. We didn't just give loans; we gave hope when the world came to a standstill. Our goal was simple: to prove that creditworthiness is not defined by assets, but by character.
2021
Bounce Back
As the second wave of the pandemic tested our resilience, we refused to let our members fall. While others paused operations, we extended a critical lifeline through flexible repayment plans and emergency credit support, ensuring our members could feed their families and keep small businesses alive during the crisis.
2022
Rapid Expansion & Recognition
Thanks to our transparent policies, we expanded to over 70+ centers. This year marked the introduction of our digital record-keeping system, reducing paperwork and speeding up loan processing times by 50%.
2023
Deepening Our Roots
A major milestone in our physical growth‚Äîwe proudly inaugurated our second branch in Khodabandpur. This expansion wasn't just about a new building; it was about accessibility. This strategic move doubled our operational capacity, allowing us to bring our services closer to thousands of unserved families close to the Samastipur district, making financial support faster and easier to access.
2024
Scaling & Modernization
We prepared for the future. We consolidated our operations across both branches and upgraded our backend infrastructure. The goal was clear: 100% transparency. We began building the architecture for the web portal we use today.
2025
The Digital Revolution
We proudly launched the "Aakanksha Group Services" web portal. This milestone allows every member to track their financial journey live on their smartphones, bringing banking transparency directly to their fingertips.

Our Values & Behaviours

We aim to conduct our businesses ethically and in a manner that is respectful to all.We achieve this by aligning our goals with our six core values.

CUSTOMER VALUE

We believe that our customers are the reason for our existence and the only guarantee to our future. Everything that we do must delight our customers, each time, and always.

OWNERSHIP MINDSET

We believe that the success and the reputation of the company is paramount. Having an ownership mindset is fundamental to our existence. It creates a sense of inspiration and purpose. It enables accountability and accomplishment. It ensures our strong commitment to the highest standards of safety and environment.

RESPECT

We champion a culture of mutual regard. We honor the unique journey of every member and employee, believing that sustainable success is only possible when every individual feels heard, valued, and understood.

INTEGRITY

Upholding our reputation is paramount as we are judged by how we act. We are committed to being truthful in all our actions. We strive to be honest and forthright with one another.

ONE TEAM

Whatever the strength of the individual, we will accomplish more together. We put the team ahead of our personal success and commit to building its capabilities.

EXCELLENCE

We are committed to excellence, in spirit and in action. We believe everything that we do and everything we think can always get better. We drive our quest for excellence always.
